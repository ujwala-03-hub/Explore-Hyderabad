Famous Malls in Hyderabad

The biggest malls in Hyderabad and what makes them stand out in terms of size, retail space, and unique design features. Hyderabad, the city of pearls, boasts some of the largest and most impressive malls in South India.

Inorbit Mall

Located in Hitech City, one of the biggest malls with shopping, dining, and entertainment.

Sarath City Capital Mall

Situated in Kondapur, it is one of the largest malls in Hyderabad offering shopping, multiplex, and events.

GVK One Mall

Located at Banjara Hills, known for its premium shopping experience.

Nexus (Sujana) Mall

Situated in Kukatpally, offers shopping, food court, and multiplex.

City Center Mall

Located in Banjara Hills, a popular mall for both shopping and leisure activities.

LuLu Mall Hyderabad

Located in Kukatpally, known for hypermarket, shopping, and dining options.

Next Galleria Mall, Punjagutta

Connected to the metro station, offers great shopping and entertainment.

Manjeera Trinity Mall

Situated in Kukatpally, offers various shopping outlets and events.

DSL Virtue Mall

Located in Uppal, a popular destination for shopping and cinema.
