Hyderabad’s streets are a treasure trove of unique finds, from traditional handicrafts to trendy fashion items. One can wander through the labyrinthine alleys of markets like Laad Bazaar, Begum Bazaar, and Charminar Bazaar, each offering its distinct flavor and array of goods.

Famous Markets

1. Laad Bazaar

Hyderabad’s culture comes alive in its bustling markets and timeless traditions. Laad Bazaar, near Charminar, is famous for its sparkling bangles, embroidered fabrics, and bridal accessories.

  1. Famous for: Bridal wear, Laad bangles, pearl jewelry, Indian wear.
  2. Location: Char Kaman, Ghansi Bazaar, Hyderabad, Telangana 500002
  3. Timings: 11:30 AM to 10:30 PM

2.Begum Bazaar

Begum Bazaar is one of the oldest and busiest markets in Hyderabad, known for its vibrant atmosphere and diverse range of products. From traditional textiles to modern fashion, this market has something for everyone. Don't forget to bargain!

  1. Famous for: Bidri work, brassware, home decor and accessories
  2. Location: Afzal Gunj, Hyderabad, Telangana 500012
  3. Timings: 10 AM to 10 PM. Closed on Sunday.

3.Sultan Bazaar

s

Hyderabad is a city full of old-world charm and rich culture. You can experience the depths of its beautiful culture by making a trip to the Koti Market. One of the Hyderabad's oldest commercial hub, the Koti Market or Sultan Bazar, is lined with quaint book shops, outlets of fancy commercial goods, heritage shops and eateries with lip-smacking food.

  1. Famous for: Fashion accessories, Indian wear
  2. Location: Koti, Hyderabad, Telangana
  3. Timings: 11 AM to 9:30 PM

4.Shilparamam

This iconic market in Hyderabad is a must visit if you love Indian art and craft. Shilparamam has a unique setup of about 15 huts depicting a rural Indian market, which is a treat for the eyes. The products that are displayed in the Artisan Bazaar are purchased directly from the artisans across the country. The wide collection of handicrafts, textile and accessories will surely surprise you.

  1. Famous for: Handicrafts, Chanderi sarees, Indian wear
  2. Entry fees: ₹40 for adults and ₹40 for children
  3. Location: Hi Tech City Main Road, Madhapur Rd, HITEC City, Hyderabad, Telangana 500081
  4. Timings: 10:30 AM to 8 PM

5. Mozamjahi Market

This is a well-known wholesale market for grains, dry-fruits, fruits and flowers. You will also find The Famous Ice Cream shop and a few famous ittar shops also nearby.

  1. Famous for: Ittar, brassware and flowers
  2. Location: Malakunta, Hyderabad, Telangana 500012
  3. Timings: 11 AM to 10 PM

6. Nampally

This is another favourite shopping place for all the bargain-hunters in Hyderabad. From bags to electronics and fabrics, you can buy anything at an incredibly low price at Nampally. This market is also the venue for the Numaish exhibition that is held every year during late January and early February.

  1. Famous for: Indian wear and electronics
  2. Location: Bazar Ghat, Nampally, Hyderabad, Telangana 500457
  3. Timings: 10 AM to 10 PM

7.Charminar Bazaar

The Sunday market in Charminar will surely stump you. It is recommended that you start your day early and enjoy a delicious breakfast from the lanes of Charminar before you go out for street shopping. You can get great deals if you are skilled at bargaining. From metal sling bags to funky jewellery and juttis, all are priced below Rs. 500.

  1. Famous for: Metal Sling bags, kharra dupatta, ethinic footwear and ittar
  2. Location: Charminar Rd, Char Kaman, Ghansi Bazaar, Hyderabad, Telangana 500002
  3. Timings: 11 AM to 10 PM

8. Abids Street

If you are searching for places to get great deals, then head over to Abdis Street. The popular store, Meena Bazaar, is located at Abids Street. It is a must visit for all your Indian fashion needs. There are plenty of other stores and street stalls that sells clothes, fancy jewellery, footwear and electronic items.

  1. Famous for: Fancy jewellery, electronics and clothes
  2. Location: Abdis Street
  3. Timings: 11 AM to 8 PM

9. Ameerpet Junction

If you adore sarees and looking for a place that will provide you with a variety of options, then Ameerpet is the perfect shopping place for you. There are a number of stores in Ameerpet Junction with the best collection of sarees in Hyderabad that will suit your taste and budget.

  1. Famous for: Sarees
  2. Location: Ameerpet Junction, Hyderabad, Telangana 500016
  3. Timings: 10 AM to 9 PM

10.Pot Market

If you are looking for traditional pottery and handicrafts, then Pot Market is the place to be. This market is known for its vibrant collection of clay pots, terracotta items, and other handmade crafts.

  1. Famous for: Pottery and handicrafts
  2. Location: Monda Market, Shivaji Nagar
  3. Timings: 10 AM to 8 PM
