Famous Restaurants in Hyderabad

\

Paradise Biryani

Paradise is synonymous with Hyderabadi Biryani. This legendary restaurant has been serving delicious biryani for decades and is a must-visit.

Bawarchi

Known for its rich and spicy biryani, Bawarchi is one of the most iconic restaurants in Hyderabad.

Shah Ghouse Cafe & Restaurant

Popular for its haleem and biryani, Shah Ghouse offers authentic Hyderabadi flavors loved by locals and tourists alike.

Chutneys

Famous for its South Indian breakfast items like idlis, dosas, and vadas, served with a variety of delicious chutneys.

Ohri's Jiva Imperia

A luxurious multi-cuisine restaurant offering Hyderabadi, North Indian, and international dishes in a royal setting.

Kritunga Restaurant

Specializes in Rayalaseema cuisine, known for its spicy dishes and authentic flavors.

Adaa at Taj Falaknuma Palace

Located in the luxurious Falaknuma Palace, Adaa serves royal Hyderabadi dishes in an opulent dining setting.

Olive Bistro

A lakeside European-style restaurant offering a romantic atmosphere and delicious continental dishes.

Jewel of Nizam – The Minar

A fine-dining restaurant known for its royal Hyderabadi cuisine and breathtaking views from its minar-style architecture.

Ulavacharu

A must-visit for authentic Andhra cuisine, famous for its spicy gongura dishes and traditional meals.

Pista House

Globally famous for its Haleem during Ramadan, Pista House also serves delicious biryani and sweets.

Mandil

Known for Middle Eastern and Mughlai cuisine, Mandil is popular for its kebabs and tandoor specials.

The Fisherman’s Wharf

A Goan-themed restaurant offering seafood delicacies and live music in a vibrant setting.

Exotica

Famous for its rooftop ambiance and North Indian cuisine, Exotica is a favorite for family dinners and romantic outings.

Barbeque Nation

A popular buffet restaurant chain offering unlimited grilled starters, mains, and desserts with live counters.

Kebabs & Kurries – ITC Kakatiya

A fine-dining restaurant specializing in Mughlai cuisine, kebabs, and North Indian delicacies.

Rayalaseema Ruchulu

A must-visit for Andhra and Rayalaseema food lovers, known for fiery spicy curries and authentic meals.

Minerva Coffee Shop

One of the oldest vegetarian restaurants in Hyderabad, known for its South Indian breakfast and meals.

Café Bahar

A popular spot for authentic Hyderabadi biryani, haleem, and kebabs at reasonable prices.

K & K – ITC Kohenur

A luxurious Mughlai and North Indian fine-dining restaurant offering royal flavors in a modern setup.

Kanak at Trident

A premium restaurant serving Indian delicacies, especially Hyderabadi cuisine in a five-star ambiance.

Hard Rock Cafe Hyderabad

Known for American-style food, burgers, music events, and a rocking ambiance.

Sahib’s Barbeque by Ohri’s

A royal-themed barbeque restaurant with live grills, Mughlai cuisine, and rich interiors.

China Bistro

A Pan-Asian restaurant serving authentic Chinese, Japanese, and Thai cuisine with stylish interiors.
