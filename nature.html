Lakes

Hussain Sagar Lake

Hyderabad offers peaceful escapes for nature lovers and leisure seekers. Hussain Sagar Lake, with its iconic Buddha statue, is perfect for evening strolls and boat rides. Nearby, Lumbini Park features musical fountains and gardens.

Osman Sagar Lake

A popular spot for panoramic views of the lake and surrounding landscape. Bring your camera—this place is a dream for golden hour shots.

Himayat Sagar Lake

Himayat Sagar Lake is a peaceful reservoir located on the outskirts of Hyderabad, offering a refreshing escape into nature 🌿. With a rating of 4.3 from over 1,500 reviews, it’s a favorite for picnics, scenic drives, and sunset views.

Durgam cheru

Durgam Cheruvu, often called the “Secret Lake,” is one of Hyderabad’s most scenic and serene nature spots 🌊. Nestled between Jubilee Hills and Madhapur, it offers a refreshing escape from the city’s bustle.

Nature Parks

Kasu Brahmanandha Reddy National Park

A peaceful oasis in Jubilee Hills, known for its peacocks, walking trails, and lush greenery. Great for morning jogs or nature photography.

Indira Park

A family-friendly park near Tank Bund with open lawns, playgrounds, and landscaped gardens. Ideal for weekend outings and casual strolls.

Krishna Kanth Park

Shaded paths, fountains, and play areas make this a relaxing spot for both kids and adults. It’s a favorite for evening walks.

Nehru Zoological Park

More than just a zoo—this massive park offers safari rides and habitats for tigers, elephants, and rhinos. A great mix of wildlife and greenery.

Mahavir Harina Vanasthali National Park

Located near Vanasthalipuram, this park is home to blackbuck antelope and offers guided safari tours. It’s Hyderabad’s largest green space.

Priyadarshini Park

A sprawling 20-acre park with sports facilities, an Olympic track, and a gym. Perfect for fitness lovers and nature seekers alike.

NTR Gardens

Set beside Hussain Sagar Lake, this park features boat rides, musical fountain shows, and a giant Buddha statue. A scenic spot for sunset views.

Oxygen Park

🍃 Oxygen Park OU Located in Osmania University, Amberpet, this park is a quiet retreat for students and locals. It opens at 4 PM and is perfect for evening walks among shaded paths and native trees.
