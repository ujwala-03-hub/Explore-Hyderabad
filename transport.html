Hyderabad Metro

Hyderabad Metro connects major IT hubs and central areas. Useful for fast travel during peak hours.

Important lines: Red (Miyapur–LBNagar), Blue (Ameerpet–Falaknuma), Green (JBS–Mahatma Gandhi Bus Station)
Timings: 06:00 — 23:00 (approx) Smart Card / Tokens

Tip: Use metro + last-mile auto/cab to reach places not directly on the line.

Rail (MMTS & Stations)

MMTS suburban trains connect Secunderabad, Lingampally, Falaknuma and more. Great for avoiding road traffic.

Major stations: Secunderabad, Hyderabad Deccan (Nampally), Falaknuma, Begumpet.

Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (RGIA)

Location: Shamshabad — ~30–40 km from city centre depending on traffic.

Transport options: Airport taxis, Ola/Uber, Airport shuttle buses (pushpak), and prepaid counters at the airport.

Autos, Cabs & Fare Tips

Autos: Negotiate short trips or insist on the meter. For 3–5 km expect ₹40–120 depending on area.

Cabs (Ola / Uber): Safer for night travel and longer trips. Use app fare estimates.

Quick FAQ

Q: Best time to travel by road?
A: Early mornings (6–9) or late evenings (after 21:00) avoid heavy traffic.

Q: Is metro cheap?
A: Yes — cheaper than cabs for many routes.
