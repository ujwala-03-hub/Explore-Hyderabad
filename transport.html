Transport in Hyderabad
<

Metro

Hyderabad Metro

Connects major IT hubs and central areas. Fast travel during peak hours.

Lines: Red (Miyapur–LB Nagar), Blue (Ameerpet–Falaknuma), Green (JBS–Mahatma Gandhi Bus Station)

Weekday Timings: 06:00 — 23:00

Weekend Timings: 07:00 — 23:00

Buses

TSRTC Buses

City and intercity buses. Bus numbers connect nearly every neighbourhood.

City Buses: Frequent & cheap. Best for short trips.

RTC Palle Velugu: Intercity / rural connectivity.

Rail & MMTS

MMTS & Stations

Suburban trains connect Secunderabad, Lingampally, Falaknuma and more.

Major stations: Secunderabad, Hyderabad Deccan, Begumpet, Falaknuma

Airport

Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

Shamshabad — ~30–40 km from city centre depending on traffic.

Transport options: Taxis, Ola/Uber, Airport shuttle buses, prepaid counters.

Autos & Cabs

Auto Rickshaws

Negotiate short trips or insist on the meter. 3–5 km: ₹40–120.

Cabs (Ola/Uber)

Safer for night travel and longer trips. Use app fare estimates.
